PaceCalc

Ignite your training.

Features

  • Add unlimited runs
  • Apple Health integration: Save runs to the Health App
  • See combined average pace for all runs
  • Metric options and conversions
  • See individual run paces
  • Calculate time from pace and distance, and distance from pace and time
  • Copy results to clipboard
  • BMR Calculations
  • Edit or delete runs after calculations
